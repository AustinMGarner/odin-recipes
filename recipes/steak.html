Steak

Home

Description

This is literally just steak, butter, and salt. I also tend to serve it with an avocado or throw an egg in at the end.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat pan with butter
  2. Once hot, add steak. Cook 2 minutes each side.
  3. Pour contents of pan onto plate and add salt.
